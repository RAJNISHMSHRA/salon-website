HEAD HUNTER SALON IS THE PERFECT PLACE TO GET AN EXCEPTIONAL HAIRSTYLE FROM TOP-NOTCH, INTERNATIONALLY TRAINED STYLISTS AND COLORISTS. SITUATED IN LOWER MANHATTAN, OUR TWO BOUTIQUE SALONS SERVE WALL STREET AND THE FINANCIAL DISTRICT, AS WELL AS BATTERY PARK AND THE WORLD TRADE CENTER AREA. WE ARE THE MOST RECOMMENDED SALON FOR HOTELS AND SPAS IN THE FINANCIAL DISTRICT AND BATTERY PARK NEIGHBORHOODS.

We offer hair services to men and women with any hair type and style -- edgy or classic, straight or curly, short or long. We will nourish your hair and the quality of your cut will last your busy lifestyle. Our bright and well-designed Downtown salons, complete with relaxing shampoo massage chairs, are a great inspiration of purity and modernity. Enjoy a glass of wine or cappuccino while we provide top-notch service.
