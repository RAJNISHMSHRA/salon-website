HEADHUNTER

HAIR STYLIST / OWNER

World renowned hair stylist,Headhunter began his career twenty years ago in southern Italy were he quickly understood great hair requires a fusion of both art and science. Headhunters eminent career then took him to Europe where he worked in salons across Paris before finally settling in the hub of NYC. In 2010 he began building his own successful brand in the financial district where he now resides with his family. Headhunter's extensive expertise and benevolent heart has not only led him to a triumphant career but to genuine and long lasting client relationships.

Headhunter is celebrated for his creative and meticulous cuts suited for each individual client.
